## DC Equipment Operations for Road Crew (v1.0.0)
### Industrial Maintenance & Pipeline Diagnostic Framework

**Developer:** Gunner Blake Carr  
**Objective:** Integrate AWS-certified welding precision with real-time data logging and equipment health diagnostics for field technicians and pipeline maintenance teams.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Weld-Log Sync
- Mobile-to-cloud synchronization of field welding parameters.
- Tracks amperage, voltage, travel speed, and weld quality metrics.
- Ensures consistency and traceability for certifications.

### 2. Diagnostic Engine
- Predictive maintenance for Lincoln and Miller welding rigs.
- Alerts technicians to potential failures before downtime occurs.
- Uses historical and real-time performance data for diagnostics.

### 3. Asset Installment Tracker
- Tracks equipment usage percentages and financial accountability.
- Enables transparency for company and individual responsibilities.
- Supports scheduling, maintenance history, and ROI calculations.

### 4. NDT Reporting
- Digital X-ray and ultrasonic testing results for pipeline inspections.
- Pass/fail logging with timestamp and inspector credentials.
- Ensures compliance with industry safety standards.
